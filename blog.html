Article 1

7 Transportation Planning Tips Before You Travel

1. Inspect and record condition: Capture photos or a short video of body panels, wheels, and windshield at pickup.

2. Confirm policy details: Validate fuel, late return, and outstation usage rules before you leave.

3. Keep support number ready: Save emergency and booking contacts for quick help during the trip.

4. Plan route in advance: Account for tolls, parking, and peak traffic windows.

5. Return on time: Timely return avoids avoidable hourly or daily overage charges.

Article 2

Airport Pickup And Drop: Quick Planning Guide

1. Choose by passengers plus luggage: One category up is better than a cramped ride for airport transfers.

2. Share precise flight info: Include terminal, flight number, and expected landing/departure time.

3. Keep 30-45 minutes buffer: It helps absorb traffic or baggage delays.

4. Confirm waiting terms: Know included waiting time and additional charges in advance.

5. Reconfirm one day before: Final confirmation reduces last-minute confusion.
