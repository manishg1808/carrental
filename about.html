Who We Are

Luxury fleet services is an Orlando based car service company - your trusted local partner for all your transit needs. We specialize in providing top-notch transportation services from Orlando Airport (MCO) directly to your hotel, Disney, Universal, Port Canaveral, and beyond. As a licensed & insured operator, we uphold the highest safety standards, reliability, and customer service. Our fleet boasts five premium cars and two spacious 14-seater vans, ensuring a perfect fit for both individual travelers and larger groups. Our professional drivers are thoroughly vetted and uniformed, promising a safe, secure, and comfortable journey every time. Choose us for a seamless and stress-free travel experience in Orlando.
